GHP Global Health Services is an elite personal and private-duty staffing provider founded on the belief that your best years don't always have to be behind you. Services

GHP Global Health Services specializes in providing private duty care programs that are tailored to an individual's specific needs. We often care for those who have post-surgery or post-hospital discharge needs. Our services are also ideal for those who face activities of daily living, lifestyle challenges and mobility difficulties related to their current health conditions.

Skilled Nursing

Direct one-on-one care for clients with a chronic illness or disability. Our Licensed and Registered Nurses will follow a care and treatment plan that is coordinated by your physician.

Home Health Aide

Experienced and Certified Aides who are also able to work under the supervision of a Registered Nurse. Our personally vetted aides pass strict background qualifications prior to hire, and are hand-matched with you to ensure a good fit.

Private Physical or Occupational Therapy

Our certifed therapists can help with rehabilitative and individualized therapy in the privacy of yours or your loved one's home, eliminating unnecessary trips to crowded rehabilitation centers.

Case Management Services

Our case management team is specifically trained to provide practical and emotional support during times of health crises. We can provide logistical support and guidance for how to handle your medical situation as well as provide referrals to physicians and additional healthcare providers as needed.

Premium Services

F.A.Q

Below are the answers to some commonly asked questions. If you have any additional questions, or if you would like to learn more about our services - Please do not hesitate to contact us via phone, email or using the form located at the end of the page.

  • Do you accept Medicare or Insurance?

    GHP does not currently accept any form of health insurance aside from Long-Term Care. We accept Check, ACH Bank Transfer, Cash as well as Credit Card (2.99% Fee for CC Transactions).

  • Do you only serve the elderly?

    Although the majority of our clients are elderly, our care team of medical professionals are able to handle a variety of cases. We can provide care to who have recently been discharged from the hospital, as well as those of all ages with disabilities such as stroke or paraplegia. Please contact us to see how we can work together for your specific needs.

  • How much do Private Duty Services cost?

    All of our packages are completely bespoke. Our services are billed on a per hour basis. Additional requests are billed à la carte. Hourly rates are dependent on factors such as location, level of care required, and level of worker chosen (Nurse or Aide). Please contact us directly for a quote.

  • Do you service areas outside of Connecticut?

    Our general service areas are the Tri-State areas of Connecticut, New York and New Jersey. However, we can accommodate clients in other areas of the country as well. Please inquire for more information.

  • What precautions are you taking for COVID-19?

    Our staff of trained medical professionals take great precautions to ensure the safety of you and your family. Our staff and contractors are required to complete a self-assessment and temperature check before each shift. As per the latest CDC guidance in healthcare settings, they are required to wear masks for the duration of their shift as well as frequently disinfect their hands and commonly touched surfaces.
