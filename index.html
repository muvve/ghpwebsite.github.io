What we do

Our Services

GHP Professional Health Services specializes in providing private duty care programs that are tailored to the needs of the homebound individual. We often care for those who have post-surgery or post-hospital discharge needs. Our services are also ideal for those who face activities of daily living challenges and mobility difficulties related to their current health conditions.



Skilled Nursing

Direct one-on-one care to clients who are homebound with a chronic illness or disability. Our Registered Nurses will follow a care and treatment plan that is coordinated by your physician.

Home Health Aide

Well-Trained and certified aides who are also able to work under the supervision of a Registered Nurse.

Private Physical or Occupational Therapy

Our therapists can help with rehabilitative and individualized therapy in the privacy of yours or your loved one's home.

Medical Social Services

We can provide emotional support during times of health crises. We can assist in referrals to additional healthcare providers and resources as needed.

International Patients


Our client care is not limited by geographical borders. We attend to International Patients and their families who come to Houston for medical care. In addition to all of our care services, we are able to assist you with:

  • Itinerary Planning for the Patient
  • Airport Pick-Up upon arrival at Houston
  • Transportation to Hotel or Desired Accommodations
  • Provide Language Interpreters
  • Accommodation Arrangements
